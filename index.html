Danilo B. Medeiros

PhD Candidate

University of Virginia

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Politics at the University of Virginia. My research explores the relationship between income inequality and legislative behavior in Brazil with a comparative perspective. My project relies heavily on ideal point estimation and time-series cross-section methods.
